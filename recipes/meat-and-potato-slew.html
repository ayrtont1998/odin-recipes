Meat and potato Slew

Uruguay is a country located in the southern cone, its geographical location means that temperatures are low, which is why many of the typical dishes, in addition to having ingredients from the region, are high in calories, ideal to be consumed in winter. Today's recipe is part of the traditional and massive Uruguayan cuisine, because in addition to being low-cost and quick and easy to prepare, it puts an end to anyone's hunger and provides energy that allows them to raise their body temperature.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of shoulder or roast beef
  • 1 big onion
  • 1 chili or pepper
  • 2 big carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 can of peas
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • Or√©gano
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Meat broth
  • 2L of Boiling water
  • 250 gram of small noodles
  • Bay leaves

Preparation

  1. Clean the meat, take it out the fat and cut in cubes.
  2. In one saucepan, put some of oil and add the onion, the chili and the cut carrots.
  3. Add salt and papper to taste
  4. Stir alternately for a few minutes so that the meat does not stick to the bottom and cooks evenly.
  5. Add the canned tomato. If it's not very crushed cut it smaller.
  6. Add the boiling water with the meat broth to taste, and the bay leaves.
  7. Let it cook at fast fire. While cut the potatoes in cubes and put it in the saucepan letting cook for 10 minutes; then add the noodles.
  8. Stir frecuently utill the noodles and potatoes are cooked.
  9. Finally add the peas and let it cook 5 minutes
  10. After this time it will be ready for serve.
