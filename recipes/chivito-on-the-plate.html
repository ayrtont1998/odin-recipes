Chivito on the Plate

Chivito is one of the most typical dishes in Uruguay, it is eaten in many ways, especially in three modalities: chivito al pan, Canadian chivito and as the main dish, it is called chivito al plato. It is one of the favorite dishes of Uruguayans and tourists, it is a beef steak, egg, ham, mozzarella cheese, French fries and Russian salad. Learn how to prepare it here, you will surely love it because it is a very complete dish both in flavor and in its nutritional content.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bife steak
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1 egg
  • ham
  • 100 gr of mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • French fries
  • Russian salad

Preparation:

  1. Fry the bacon and a few slices of onion
  2. In the same pan and with the fat of the bacon, cook the bife, adding to it a bit of salt when it's halfdone and also adding the ham and the mozzarella on top
  3. Fry the egg, using a clean pan if it needed
  4. Cut the tomato in slices y start to prepare the plate in the next way: put the bife with the ham and mozzarella on the top in the plate, then add the bacon, tomato, fried egg, and onion on the top (you can change the order). Add some more tomato and lettuce beside, with the french fries and the russian salad.
